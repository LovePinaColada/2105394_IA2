Theme of the Site

Intended Audience

The intended audience for this website is any college student interested in web design and programming. It aims to provide valuable insights into the world of web development,
offering inspiration and guidance for building an impressive portfolio. Additionally, the website targets companies seeking potential hires by showcasing the creator's skills and work.



Colours Used

The chosen color scheme for the website consists of black, white, orange, and blue.
These colors are carefully selected to create a visually appealing and professional look while ensuring high contrast and readability.



Pictures and Videos

To enhance the aesthetic appeal of the website, a variety of pictures and videos are thoughtfully integrated.
These visual elements not only make the content engaging but also contribute to the overall user experience, making the site more captivating and informative.
